Cleopatra Degher is a speech therapist, singer-songwriter, and picture book writer. Her music has been featured on NPR's World Cafe Next, and the Austin Chronicle called her "the Emmylou Harris for a new generation of folk." She grew up in Sweden and now lives in California with her husband and toddler.
